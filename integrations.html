How integrations work

Live, audited, and operator-controlled.

Every integration runs through the same safety architecture: pre-test on credential save, encrypted-at-rest credentials, idempotency-keyed pushes, and full audit trail.

Connect

Save credentials via concierge OHIP setup or self-serve admin form. ARIO immediately pre-tests the credentials against the partner's health endpoint and stores them encrypted.

Sync

Nightly data sync pulls statistics, pace, segment mix, and group blocks. Hourly incremental sync keeps fresh data flowing into the decision layer.

Push

Outbound rate / restriction / availability pushes are queued, safety-gated, parity-checked, magnitude-clamped, and idempotency-keyed before reaching the partner API.
